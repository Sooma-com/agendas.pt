About Agendas.pt

Agendas.pt is a self-hostable scheduling service by Sooma Labs — connect a calendar, publish bookable meeting types, and share a link.

Open source & licence

Agendas.pt is built on cal.rs, an independent open-source scheduling platform created by Olivier Lambert, and is distributed under the GNU Affero General Public License, version 3 (AGPL-3.0).

In accordance with the AGPL, the complete source code of this service — including our modifications — is publicly available:

  • Upstream cal.rs: github.com/olivierlambert/calrs
